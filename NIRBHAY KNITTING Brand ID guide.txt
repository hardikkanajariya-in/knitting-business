BRAND STORY (SHORT VERSION)
Use this in presentations, brochures, website intros.
For over 40 years, Nirbhay Knitting has engineered technical textiles that serve as the foundation for 
high-performance coated and laminated materials. From 3D spacer fabrics to coated-ready knits and 
raised substrates, our materials are designed to perform consistently on coating and lamination lines 
across Europe.
With zero-discharge manufacturing, waterless finishing, and a commitment to clean, responsible 
production, we combine engineering precision with sustainable innovation.
We don’t just supply fabrics — we build long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and 
technical excellence.
VISION & MISSION (BRAND-ALIGNED)
Vision
We envision a global market where engineered fabrics are not commodities, but trusted 
components that elevate product quality, reduce environmental impact, and unlock new possibilities 
across industries. We are here to shape the future of technical textiles by creating sustainable, 
high-performance materials that enable our customers to innovate with confidence. 
Mission
Our mission is to design and deliver advanced knitted and coated textiles through responsible 
manufacturing, deep material expertise, and continuous innovation. We combine four decades of 
craftsmanship with modern technology to provide customized, reliable and sustainable solutions for 
automotive, footwear, home furnishings and protective applications — supporting our customers as 
a long term partner in performance, quality and growth.

BRAND VALUES
Engineering First
Every fabric is designed with purpose, precision, and performance in mind.
Consistency Above All
Predictable substrates reduce waste, improve yield, and increase efficiency.
Sustainability as Standard
Zero-discharge, waterless finishing, clean energy — not optional, but essential.
Partnership Over Transaction
We grow with our customers, not at their expense.
Continuous Improvement
40 years of learning, refining, and innovating.
